Memory Verse

“Take ye heed, watch and pray: for ye know not when the time is.” - Mark 13:33

Introduction

Watchfulness is the state of being constantly attentive...

Outlines

1. BE PREPARED

  • Live righteously (Psalm 24:3-4)
  • Study and obey God's word (Psalm 119:11)
  • Consistent prayer and fasting (Luke 21:36)
  • Emotional stability (Romans 8:14)
  • Fill your spirit with songs and hymns (Ephesians 5:19)
  • Evangelise (Mark 16:15)
  • Forgive and live peaceably (Colossians 3:12-14)
  • Stay humble (Matthew 18:4)
  • Be mindful of kingdom business (1 Thessalonians 2:8)
  • Fellowship with brethren (Hebrews 10:25)

2. BE CAUTIOUS

  • Watch your words (Matthew 12:36)
  • Guard your actions (Titus 2:7-8)
  • Watch your thoughts (Proverbs 23:7)
  • Work on your character (2 Timothy 3:1-6)
  • Eliminate bad habits (1 Corinthians 10:31)
  • Avoid overconfidence (1 Corinthians 10:12)
  • Beware of deceivers (Mark 13:5)

Class Activities

1: What else can you do to be prepared for heaven?

2: How do you feel about people with negative energy around you?

Conclusion

Watch out for vices capable of hindering you from making the rapture. Deal with them before it is too late.

Questions

  • Mention five areas believers should be prepared.
  • Identify four areas of caution for believers.

Assignment

Mention five ways you have been preparing for the second coming of the Lord (2x5=10 Marks).
